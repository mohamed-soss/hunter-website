Our Credentials

In 2007, with a passion for exceptional market research, Ramy Soliman began his journey as a field executive for one of the globally leading Market Research Companies. Hunter International is a firm that prides itself on precise, client-tailored insights. Under Ramy's leadership, Hunter has flourished, earning a reputation for innovative methods and robust analysis.
